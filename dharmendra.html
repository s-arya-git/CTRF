Dr. Dharmendra Jain

Ph.D

jain_dharmendra2004@hotmail.com

"CONVENTIONAL CYTOGENETICS SPECIALIST"

Dr Dharmendra Jain is presently working as Post-Doctoral Scholar, Section of Transplant, Department of Surgery, University of Chicago, Illinois USA. Dr Dharmendra is specialised in field of conventional cytogenetic, Fluorescent In Situ Hybridisation, Polymerase Chain Reaction and flowcytometry.

His total engrossment in the basic research of the disease and its understanding in diagnostics has led to numerous well renowned national and international publications in his name. He is also awarded with Harold Gunson Fellowship for oral presentation of the research work "Detection of Donor T and B Cells Specific c4d Fixing Allo-antibodies using Flow Cytometrv: A novel diagnostic approach" at 25th regional congress of ISBT, London, UK. To add more feathers in cap he has multiple patents filed on his name, is an invited speaker in national conference and has various oral presentations in national conferences.

Dr. Dharmendra has previously worked as Senior Scientific Officer at Molecular Genetics Lab Medanta The Medicity. He was also associated with reputed institutes like Apollo Indraprastha and Dabur's Onquest New Delhi. He has completed his PhD from Amity Institute of Biotecnology Noida New Delhi and Msc Biotechnology from Barkatullah Vishwavidhyalaya, Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. Dr Dharmendra holds a key position in analysing critically every aspect of research project and advising about the novel techniques.
