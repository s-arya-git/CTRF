swatimittal@intellilocus.com

"REGISTERED INDIAN PATENT AND TRADEMARK AGENT"

Swati Mittal is the Managing Partner and founder of Intellilocus IP Services. She is a Registered Indian Patent and Trademark Agent and holds a Masters Degree in Biotechnology, along with a PG Diploma in Patents Law from NALSAR Hyderabad and MBA in Marketing from IMT Ghaziabad.

She has been practicing IP since 2011 and is consulting numerous organizations including startups and SMES for expanding their business horizons. She has also been an avid speaker and taken a number of sessions at startup events Like Rajasthan Startup Fest and various universities on the topic of IP. She has drafted numerous patents in the field of software, and healthcare. Her portfolio also includes trademark filings for brands across India.

Now she shares her passion and knowledge through Intellilocus to rekindle the fire of innovation in every mind and help every individual organisation maximize the potential of their intellectual property which includes trademarks, copyrights, designs and patents. She has also pioneered an education movement by conducting various IP awareness campaigns and events. Through her IP Academy she has been empowering individuals to make a career in the field of IP and also guiding entrepreneurs on all aspects of IP.
